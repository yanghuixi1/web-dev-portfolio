I'm moved to bring data to life - taking it from an initial scrape to a robust model, with a special focus on sequence data: natural language and time series. I have abundant experience in generating primary data as well as aggregating secondary data from disparate sources and translating it into value for the organizations that I've been a part of.

Throughout my career, I've had the fortune of working in several countries and collaborating with diverse teams that have shaped my ability to adapt and thrive quickly in new environments. As a researcher I uncover the gears that drive complex systems and as a teacher I distill them into practical understanding from which concrete action steps can be taken. Now I seek to leverage what I've built to tackle more challenging problems and produce greater impact through my work in future-minded organizations that put data at the forefront of their product and processes.
